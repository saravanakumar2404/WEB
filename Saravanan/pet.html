WELCOME TO THE URBAN HOUND

The Urban Hound is Boston's premier full-service dog care facility. We offer daycare, boarding,
walking, grooming, and training. Located in the South End of Boston, we are easy to access from
93, The Mass Pike, and Logan Airport. With certified dog trainers on staff and 24-hour supervised
care 365 days a year, you know your dog is always in a safe and in a loving environment.

Become an Urban Hound today, and see what all the WOOF is about!
Call us at (617) 755-5775 for more info.
